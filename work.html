What I do

I am an aspiring product design engineer with a focus on electro-mechanical components. I strive to deliver efficient and precision designs through the techniques of GD&T.

At university, I am developing a regeneratively cooled liquid rocket engine as a member of Northeastern's Aerospace Club.

Featured Work:

Bowing Analysis in ANSYS

I developed ANSYS mechanical simulations to determine if the specified pre-load on flange bolts was sufficient to correct observed bowing in production hardware. I am currently learning and utilizing ANSYS Electronics Desktop to perform capacitance studies.
