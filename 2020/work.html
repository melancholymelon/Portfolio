AerospaceNU

I developed ANSYS mechanical simulations to determine if the specified pre-load on flange bolts was sufficient to correct observed bowing in production hardware. I have also made ANSYS Maxwell simulations to analyze induced magnetic fields.



In addition, I designed and tested new manufacturing processes for next generation intertial guidance hardware, and led analysis into current manufacturing practices as well as in current testing procedures.
